About us

Leveraging AI for Simplified Civic Information

CivicaAI is a Nigerian civic tech platform dedicated to democratizing access to civic information. We believe that an informed citizenry is the backbone of a strong democracy. Our platform is designed to simplify complex information into easily digestible formats, bridging the gap between citizens and the governance process.

Our mission is to empower citizens with accessible and accurate civic information, promote transparency, and enhance civic participation.
